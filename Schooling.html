My School Life

Introduction

My name is Aarya Patel, I studied in St. Joseph's public school, King Koti branch, Hyderabad. I joined in SJPS in June,2008. I joined here in LKG(2008) and completed my 10th in February,2020. OMG! I was there for almost 12 years, i can't believe that i have spent a whole lot of 12 years at that place. School life is one of the most cherished phases of life. It is a journey filled with learning, friendship, fun, and valuable life lessons.

Early Years

My schooling began at a lively elementary school. I still remember the first day—nervous yet excited to step into a world of learning. I knew nothing except daily going to school and coming half-day crying in search on Mom. Initially, I used to think that they have left me alone here and went home. I had no choice other than making friends and enjoy the pripratory school. I never thought that, this is the plcae where i will making friends for life. Early years in school was truly Memorable.

Middle School Adventures

As I progressed into middle school, my interests expanded. Sports, computer labs, and friendship. So, these were perticularly my area of interests. I would call myself lucky because, i made friends not only in my class, but rather in other sections as well. Our school total had 10 sections for each class. I knew almost everyone from each section. Each section had almost around 55-60 students. So, almost total strength of each class would be around 500 students. This was the place where we made friends not for a reason nor having to expect something in return. It was an open minded friendship not expecting something in return. But honestly, I must say, we all had great unity when it comes to bunking classes and playing outdoord, or cracking jokes. It was truly lots of fun:)

High School Challenges and Achievements

High school was a mix of challenges and triumphs. From handling academic pressure to making lifelong friends, it shaped me into who I am today. Every school day followed a routine: Morning prayers, classes, lunch breaks, and games. Each moment contributed to unforgettable experiences. Bonding with friends is something that was very important and that i build over there. Our batch was lucky enough because, we comleted our 10th and immediately that was the time when the COVID-19 virus enters India and we have to face "The Lockdown".

Memorable Events

  • Being a back bencher
  • Participating in a school Annual cultural festival
  • Going on a fun excursion trip
  • Farewell day — A bittersweet moment

Friends and Teachers

My school friends were my second family. Our shared memories, laughter, and challenges made school life even more special.

Teachers played a crucial role in guiding us. Their encouragement and wisdom shaped my outlook on education and life.

Sports and Extracurricular Activities

Sports days were always thrilling. Competing in races and various other matches made school even more exciting.

Beyond academics, I participated in debates, competitions, and role play performances, building confidence along the way.

Conclusion

Though my school journey has ended, the memories and experiences will remain with me forever. School life was truly a golden period that I will always cherish. If there is still something that i could ask for, is to bring back my school life again. Hence, i just want those days back in my life again.
